3D Printed RC Car

Designed in Solidworks

This is a rc car I designed and 3d printed, It consistes of roughly 40 uniquely modeled parts.

The only components that are not 3d printed on this is the hardware, steering linkages,tires,shocks and the electronics

Car is rear wheel drive and driven with a 1000kv motor connected to a gearbox with a approximately 2 to 1 gear reduction to the wheels.

I plan to revist this project and make a second iteration with larger shockes allowing for more suspension travel and slighlty improve the steering mechanics by adding a few degrees of castor to the front wheels
